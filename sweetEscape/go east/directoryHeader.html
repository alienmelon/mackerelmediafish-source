You head east...

Before you stands a sign aptly pointing to the east village!

This looks promising...

Although something seems wrong... You can't quite put your finger on it yet. Maybe it's just the pronounced prejudice against frogs. The sign clearly indicates that they are not welcome in the village. That's too bad. There are a lot of frogs on this server.

Just up ahead you see smoke. Maybe you should check it out?

(Still, there's plenty to see on this server. You have a feeling you should visit the East Village only if you've finished exploring.)
