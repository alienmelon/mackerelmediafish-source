Approaching the dark cave...
The road you are on comes to an end before a very dark, very foreboding cave. A ghastly howl echoes from it. Maybe it's the wind... maybe something more sinister.
Either way, you are sure that something has made its home here.
Perhaps it's best to turn around and not disturb whatever lies beyond the dark...
