Far away from the wizard of the north...

You leave the wizard behind. You can hear him already asking another traveler the same question... Poor soul.

The unicode landscape is beautiful here. You can see rugged hills just ahead, and beneath them is a cave to the east.
The ASCII hills look inviting.

As you take a closer look you notice that the ground is dotted with frogs. There are literally hundreds of them here.
It dawns on you that they must be the travelers that failed to answer the wizard correctly. Obviously he has been at this for a while.
The frogs look friendly! Some appear accessible. Maybe they can aid you on your quest with worthwhile information?

You hear a magical zap in the distance! Another traveler has just been turned into a frog...
